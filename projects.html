Current Projects

We are proud to present Line of Command, a spiritual successor to the acclaimed turn based tactical Gameboy Advance game series.

Line of Command has a working computer player and a small campaign. The graphics is totally custom, based on Simple Fast Media Library (SFML).

We also have Map Maker, a procedural map generator for creating a grid-world with earth-like terrain and outrageously named countries inhabiting it.
