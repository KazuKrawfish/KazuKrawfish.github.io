Map Maker

Map Maker is a procedural map generator which creates a grid-world with Earth-like terrain. It randomly determines elevation, temperature and precipitation using random noise. It then populates that world with procedurally generated provinces and countries, each with a name more outrageous than the last.

Additionally, population growth and the spread of trade and technology can be tracked turn by turn. The intent is to simulate the industrial-era globalization.

Looking forward I would like to build in more complicated behaviors for the countries, including war and a more detailed model for economic development and exploitation of natural resources, including potentially making a single player economic/business sim.
